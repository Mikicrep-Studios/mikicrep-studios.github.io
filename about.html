Mikicrep Studios - About Us

Mikicrep Studios was founded on August 19, 2021, with a mission to create high-quality, free, and open-source software (FOSS) for everyone. Our team is passionate about technology and innovation, and we strive to contribute to the open-source community by developing tools and applications that are accessible to all.

At Mikicrep Studios, we believe in the power of collaboration and the importance of sharing knowledge. Our projects are designed to be user-friendly and customizable, allowing developers and users to adapt them to their specific needs. We are committed to maintaining transparency in our development process and welcome contributions from the community.

Our founder is an avid supporter of open-source software and is dedicated to promoting its use and development. We are proud to use Arch Linux as our primary operating system, which reflects our commitment to flexibility, simplicity, and user control.

Join us on our journey to create innovative software solutions that empower users and developers alike. Together, we can build a better, more open digital world.
